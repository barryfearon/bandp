Professional Painter and Decorators in the Isle of Man

Buchannan & Pitts Painting and Decorating has been providing painting and decorating services in and around the Isle of Man since 1985. You will not find a more established and respected painter and decorator in Bedford for all of your home painting and decorating needs. With over 34 years of experience in the decorating trade and an outstanding selection of testimonials you can be assured by using George Coull you will have one of the best painters and decorators around working on your property. I cover all interior and exterior painting and decorating work from a complete house redecoration to painting a single ceiling, no job is too big or too small. I take great pride in of all the work I carry out, I ensure all my customers homes are always left clean and tidy and are 100% satisfied before any payment is due.

I provide an outstanding and professional service to all of my customers. Trust is a huge factor when allowing tradesmen into your home but its something I pride myself on and is one of the reasons why I have so many returning clients and recommendations. As you can see by reading all my genuine 5 star reviews on Google and Yell.com just how highly customers rate me as a person and the standard of my work. I work for some customers that go to work all day long and they ask me to lock up when I leave, I have worked for customers while they have been away on holiday and I have been the key holder for the property, this is trust which is earned and built over years of experience in the decorating trade. I treat all customers properties the way I would expect tradesmen to treat my house if I required any work carrying out.

So if I can help in any way with advice or if you are looking for a painter and decorator in the Isle of Man please get in touch for a totally Free quotation on a day and time that suits yourself.
